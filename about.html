ABOUT US

BMR Realty Services is an elite property monitoring, management and consulting firm founded with a spirit of integrity and partnership with our team, and most importantly, our clients. Our highly skilled team has performed hundreds of property assessments for corporations, large and small. We have helped our clients obtain a peace of mind with monitoring and securing their property. But most importantly, each team member has been trusted to provide ongoing and reliable property advisory solutions to our client's evolving security and business needs.

Our mission

Delivering trustworthy experiences that you cherish for a lifetime. We aim to establish a reliable partnership with you to protect your property and as your trusted property advisors, we specialize in identifying threats that may exploit your property. We work with you to develop a reliable plan for remediation and to ultimately implement an in-depth defence strategy.

Our vision

To be the first choice for our consumers and partners in their journey. We do that with data, design, technology, and above all the passion of our people while delivering value to our shareholders.
